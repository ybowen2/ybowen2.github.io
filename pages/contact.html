THE VAGINA MONOLOGUES

({DONATE})

The Vagina Monologues UCSD donates 100% of our proceeds to nonprofits working toward fighting sexual violence and gender inequity each year. In the past several years, our nonprofit has been the Center for Community Solutions (CCS), the only rape crisis center in all of San Diego County. During the 2015-16 season, we raised over $10,000 for CCS.

If you’re interested in donating to the Vagina Monologues, please contact us at vdayucsd@gmail.com and we’ll provide you with our 501(c) tax-exempt number.

({JOIN US})

If you want to get involved, you should definitely follow us on Facebook, Instagram, and/or our website for updates. We begin auditions between September-October each year.
